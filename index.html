Rodent Problems

Rodents like rats and mice are not only a nuisance but can also pose significant health risks and cause considerable damage to your property. These agile pests can squeeze through incredibly small openings, gaining access to your home or business from various entry points. Once inside, they can contaminate food, spread diseases through their droppings and urine, and gnaw on electrical wires, potentially creating fire hazards. The presence of rodents is a serious concern that requires prompt and effective action. Their ability to reproduce rapidly means that a small problem can quickly escalate into a full-blown infestation if not addressed promptly. Beyond the health and safety risks, rodents can also cause structural damage by chewing through walls, insulation, and even pipes. Recognizing the signs of a rodent infestation is crucial for timely intervention. These signs can include finding droppings, hearing scratching noises in walls or attics, or discovering gnaw marks on food packaging or structures. Ignoring a rodent problem will not make it go away; in fact, it will likely worsen over time.
